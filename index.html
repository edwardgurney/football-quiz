The Football Quiz World Cup

The Rules

You have 60 seconds to get through both rounds. The first round is the group stage of the cup.

You will have six questions and you recieve 3 points for each correct answer.

You need 12 points to qualify from the group stages and progress to the knockout stage.

It's now the knockout stages and you start in the quarter final

One incorrect answer and you're out... can you go all the way to the final and lift the cup?
